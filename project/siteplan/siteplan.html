Sunny Side Up Golf Course - Site Plan

Site Name

Sunny Side Up Golf Course

Sunny Side Up Golf Course was chosen because it gives the feeling of a fresh start and optimism, which is important for golfers looking for a positive experience. It also suggests early morning tee times when the sun is rising, which is often the best time to play golf.

Site Purpose

The Sunny Side Up Golf Course website serves as the primary online presence for our golf facility. The site aims to:

  • Showcase our beautiful 18-hole course through interactive hole-by-hole previews
  • Provide an easy-to-use tee time booking system for members and guests
  • Display a gallery of course photos highlighting our well-maintained greens and facilities
  • Inform visitors about course amenities, clubhouse features, and available services
  • Share information about membership options, tournaments, and special events

The website will be designed to attract new golfers while serving as a valuable resource for regular players and members.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

"What are the green fees, and can I book a tee time online?"

Visitors will find our online booking system featured on the homepage, with clear pricing information and availability calendar.

Scenario 2

"I've heard this course has some challenging holes. Can I see what the course looks like before I play?"

Visitors will benefit from our interactive hole-by-hole previews showing the layout of both front and back nine holes with descriptions of each hole's features and challenges.

Scenario 3

"Does the clubhouse have dining options or a pro shop where I can purchase equipment?"

Visitors will find detailed information on our "About" page describing all clubhouse amenities, including our restaurant, pro shop inventory, and other services available.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E7D32 (Forest Green)

Secondary Color: #FFC107 (Sunny Yellow)

Accent Color: #1565C0 (Water Blue)

Neutral Color: #F5F5F5 (Off-White)

Color Usage

  • Forest Green (#2E7D32): Primary color for headers, navigation, and footer. Represents the lush fairways and natural setting of the golf course.
  • Sunny Yellow (#FFC107): Used for call-to-action buttons, highlights, and accents. Represents sunshine and the "Sunny Side Up" brand identity.
  • Water Blue (#1565C0): Used for links, secondary buttons, and water hazard representations in course maps. Represents water features on the course.
  • Off-White (#F5F5F5): Background color for content areas, providing a clean, easy-to-read canvas.

Wireframes

Mobile View

The mobile view prioritizes easy navigation and booking functionality, with the carousel displays stacked vertically for optimal viewing on smaller screens.

Desktop View
